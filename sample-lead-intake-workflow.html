This is one sample workflow from the AI Workflow Fix Kit. The full kit includes inbox-to-task routing, meeting follow-ups, review request drafts, prompt templates, a buyer handout, and an implementation checklist.

Workflow

  1. A new lead arrives from a form, email, or booking page.
  2. The lead is added to a spreadsheet or CRM.
  3. AI summarizes the request and classifies urgency.
  4. AI drafts a reply for human review.
  5. The owner reviews, edits, and sends.
